About us

Forced Fund is small niched NGO based in Kyiv.

So called “family fund”, except we are not family but just a bunch of friends who woke up early at 24 February 2022 with heavy bombardment of Kyiv. The ugly war has began and four of us where forced to act. Forced not by law but principles and love to Ukraine and our nation. And fear, sure thing.

What we do

We perform information analysis, researching, procurements, logistics and direct delivery to the frontline

Currently we operate in South Eastern Ukraine in Kharkiv, Dnipro, Zaporizhzha, Mykolaiv regions. We keep in touch directly with battalions command and gaining fresh and real request from the frontline, doing our best to execute them ASAP.
